# Rahul Pharma Government Tender Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview

Rahul Pharma participates in government medicine tenders across India.

This project was developed to provide management with a centralized dashboard for monitoring:

- Tender Performance
- Inventory Management
- Supplier Performance
- Delivery Performance
- Outstanding Payments

---

## Business Problem

Management lacked a centralized reporting solution to monitor tender performance, operational efficiency and financial performance.

As a result:

- Tender performance was difficult to track
- Supplier delays impacted deliveries
- Inventory risks were not visible
- Outstanding payments were difficult to monitor

---

## Project Objective

Develop an interactive Power BI dashboard to support business decisions across:

1. Executive Management
2. Procurement Team
3. Logistics Team
4. Finance Team
5. Tender Team

---

## Key Business Insights

### Executive Dashboard

- Tender win ratio remains above 70%.
- Maharashtra generates the highest revenue.
- Emergency category performance requires improvement.

### Operations Dashboard

- Delivery delays remain the biggest operational challenge.
- Maharashtra records the highest delayed deliveries.
- Capsule and Tablet categories show higher expiry risk.

### Finance Dashboard

- Outstanding payments are concentrated among a few hospitals.
- ESIC Hospital has the highest outstanding amount.
- Maharashtra contributes the highest receivable value.

---

## Business Recommendations

- Improve bidding strategy for low-performing categories.
- Strengthen logistics planning in high-delay regions.
- Reduce inventory expiry risk.
- Prioritize collection efforts for high-value hospitals.
- Monitor outstanding payments regularly.
